Who We Are

Our Story

Pacific Edge Pools was built on a simple principle: do the job right the first time. With over 15 years of hands-on experience in high-end pool remodeling, replastering, and resurfacing, our team brings luxury-level craftsmanship to homeowners throughout the Central Valley and Northern California.

Our background includes working in elite markets such as Carmel and Atherton, where attention to detail, precision, and professionalism are non-negotiable. Today, we apply that same standard to every pool remodeling and replastering project we take on — no shortcuts, no guesswork.

Whether you're in Modesto, Stockton, Sacramento, Livermore, or San Jose, you get the same quality, the same communication, and the same commitment to doing it right.
